Having Technology Problems?
Tech support is available each day on campus from 8:15 am - 9:15 am Monday - Friday and in the afternoon from 1:50 pm - 4:15 pm on Mondays and from 2:30 pm - 4:15 pm on Tuesday through Friday. Please do not come to campus for tech support during Period 1-4 or Advisory time.
